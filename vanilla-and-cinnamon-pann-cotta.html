Vanilla and cinnamon panna cotta

Description

This easy panna cotta uses shop-bought youghurt and passionfruit

Ingredients

  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 cups 99% fat-free diet vanilla yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 1 tbsp gelatine powder
  • 2 passionfruit, halved

Steps

  1. Combine milk and cinnamon in a small saucepan over low heat. Bring to the boil, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Allow to cool to room temperature. Remove cinnamon sticks.
  2. Combine milk mixture and yoghurt in a large bowl. Pour boiling water into a jug. Sprinkle over gelatine. Using a fork, whisk until gelatine is dissolved. Stir through yoghurt mixture until smooth.
  3. Divide panna cotta mixture between four 3/4 cup-capacity moulds. Place on a tray. Cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 6 hours or until firm.
  4. Carefully run a round-bladed knife around the edge of each panna cotta. Turn moulds out onto plates. Drizzle with passionfruit pulp. Serve.
